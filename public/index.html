CONTROLS

Create Attacker:
Press "A" to create your attacker

Choose Path:
-Press "1" for middle
-Press "2" for top
-Press "3" for bottom

Create Defender
Right click where you would like to place your defender. Defender will only last 30 seconds

GOAL

Mission:
The other player is trying to cut off your precious locks of hair! Defend yourself by placing turrets and sending out some attackers of your own. First player to collect all of the other player's hair wins!

Resource Points:
-Defenders cost 3
-Attackers cost 2

Time:
1 additional point will be given every 10 seconds
