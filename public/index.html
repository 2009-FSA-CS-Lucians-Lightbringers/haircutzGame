CONTROLS

Create Attacker and Choose Path:
-Press "1" to send down middle path
-Press "2" to send down top path
-Press "3" to send down bottom path

Create Defender
Left click where you would like to place your defender. Defender will only last 30 seconds

GOAL

Mission:
The other player is trying to cut off your precious locks of hair! Defend yourself by placing turrets and sending out some attackers of your own. First player to collect all of the other player's hair wins!

Resource Points:
-Defenders cost 3
-Attackers cost 2

Time:
1 additional point will be given every 10 seconds
